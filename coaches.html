Our Coaching Methodology

At Strikers Elite Academy, we combine European training techniques with African football dynamics to create complete strikers who excel in any system. Our coaching philosophy focuses on:

  • Individualized technical development
  • Tactical intelligence training
  • Position-specific conditioning
  • Mental toughness coaching
  • Game intelligence development

Coach Qualifications

All our head coaches hold minimum:

  • UEFA B License (or equivalent)
  • 5+ years professional experience
  • First Aid & Sports Medicine certification
  • Continuing education requirements
